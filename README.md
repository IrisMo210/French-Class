# 🇫🇷 La Classe de Français

**Our group French class app — objectif : DELF A2 !**

👉 **Open the app:** https://irismo210.github.io/French-Class/

A self-contained web app for our French class. It works on any phone or computer,
needs no account and no installation — your progress is saved automatically on
your own device.

---

## How we learn (two layers)

**1. On your own phone — whenever you want.**
Pick your name, choose a lesson, and practise at your own speed. Fast learners
can run ahead of the class; others can simply follow the classes. Every lesson has:

| Tab | What it trains |
|---|---|
| **Apprendre** | The lesson's words — every noun with its gender (blue = masculine, rose = feminine), pronunciation, 🔊 audio |
| **Quiz** | English → type the French (article + word) |
| **Écoute** | Listen without reading → choose the meaning → repeat aloud |
| **Genre** | un or une? Gender training |
| **Phrases** | Translate full sentences |
| **Conjugaison** | Present-tense verb forms |
| **Dialogue** | A real scene in 3 levels: ① listen & read → ② choose your reply → ③ type your own reply |

Plus **🔁 Révision mixte** on the home screen — 10 quick questions across
everything you've studied; the words *you* get wrong come back more often.

**2. Together in class — every day.**
Our two French-speaking teachers run the class using **🎓 Mode Classe** on the
shared class device: big-screen slides with the day's words (listen & repeat
together), the grammar point, the dialogue to act out in pairs, and speaking
activities. No drills in class — those are homework. Teachers move slides with
the ← → arrow keys and advance the class lesson when the group is ready.

## The plan

- **40 lessons** following the real chapters of the three *Vocabulaire
  Progressif du Français* books (CLE International): Débutant Complet A1.1 →
  Débutant A1 → Intermédiaire A2/B1.
- **An exam every 5 lessons** (8 total), in the real **DELF A2 format**:
  4 parts × 25 points (listening · reading · writing · speaking), pass at
  **50/100** with at least 5/25 in every part — so exam day feels familiar.
- Goal: everyone passes **DELF A2 in about 5 months**.

## Your progress & privacy

- Progress is stored **only on your own device** (browser storage). Nothing is
  sent anywhere; there are no accounts.
- Several people can share one device — everyone taps their own name
  (**+ ajouter un nom** / **– retirer**).
- **Sauvegarder** gives you a backup code; **Restaurer** brings your progress
  onto a new phone. Keep the code somewhere safe!
- One warning: clearing your browser's data erases your progress — export a
  backup code from time to time.

## Tips

- 📱 **iPhone/Android:** open the link in the browser and use *Add to Home
  Screen* — it then behaves like a normal app, offline too.
- 🔊 The voice is your phone's built-in French voice. On iPhone the very first
  ▶ tap can be silent — tap once more.
- 🤔 Stuck on a word? Every drill has an *"I don't know — show me"* button.
  Using it is learning, not cheating.

## About the content

The lesson structure follows the chapter order of the *Vocabulaire Progressif
du Français* series, which the class studies from — please buy and use the
actual books for the full lessons and exercises; they're excellent. All
practice content in this app (examples, dialogues, exams) is original. Exam 8
is taken on paper in class with the official *Le DELF A2 100% réussite* mock
exams and their real audio.

New lessons and exams are added as the class advances — the class asks our
tutor (Claude) to *« préparer la leçon N »*.
